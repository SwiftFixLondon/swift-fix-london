Warranty Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Warranty Coverage

Swift Fix London provides a 90-day warranty on replacement parts installed during repair services.

Warranty Applies To

  • Defective replacement screens
  • Defective replacement batteries
  • Defective charging port replacements

Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Physical damage
  • Liquid damage
  • Accidental damage
  • Unauthorised repairs by third parties
  • Normal wear and tear

Warranty Claims

Please contact us within the warranty period with details of the issue.
